# Getting Started with InsightFlow

This tutorial will guide you through your first end-to-end data analysis workflow using InsightFlow.

## Step 1: Uploading Data
To begin, upload one or more datasets to the platform. InsightFlow supports CSV, Excel, or database exports.

## Step 2: Review the Data Profile
Once uploaded, InsightFlow will automatically process your data and present a **Data Profiling** report. Review the summary to understand the overall health of your data, including missing values, duplicates, and column data types.

## Step 3: Apply Cleaning Recommendations
Navigate to the **Data Cleaning Assistant**. InsightFlow will suggest actions to fix issues identified in the profiling stage. Apply recommendations to standardize formats, handle missing values, and remove duplicates to prepare a clean dataset.

## Step 4: Generate a Data Model
If you uploaded multiple tables, go to the **Data Modeling Assistant**. Review the suggested relationships (primary and foreign keys) and accept the recommended Star or Snowflake schema structure.

## Step 5: Explore DAX & Visualizations
With a clean data model, InsightFlow will automatically generate a list of recommended **DAX Measures** and **Visualizations**. You can now use these recommendations to build out your Power BI dashboard or explore insights directly using the **AI Analytics Assistant**.
