# Problem Statement

Data analytics is a powerful tool for organizations, but the process of extracting actionable insights from raw data is often fraught with inefficiencies and technical hurdles.

Traditionally, analysts face several bottlenecks:
- **Data Understanding and Profiling**: Significant time is spent just understanding the structure, types, and anomalies within a newly acquired dataset.
- **Data Quality Issues**: Detecting and resolving missing values, duplicates, and outliers is a manual, error-prone, and repetitive process.
- **Complex Data Modeling**: Creating robust relationships between tables (such as Star or Snowflake schemas) requires deep technical knowledge and careful planning.
- **Advanced Metric Calculation**: Writing accurate Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) for Power BI or similar BI tools can be highly complex and intimidating for business users.
- **Visualization Selection**: Choosing the *right* chart to represent a specific type of data often involves trial and error, leading to suboptimal dashboards.

InsightFlow was built to eliminate these bottlenecks by automating the manual preparation, modeling, and metric generation phases, allowing users to focus purely on deriving and acting upon business insights.
